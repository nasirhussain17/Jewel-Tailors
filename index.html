About Us

Excellence. Elegance. Glory - Jewel Tailors There are many bespoke tailors who do what we do. We maintain a tradition of excellence when it comes to clothing . We are Patna's one of the leading tailors with a firm commitment, extraordinary quality and exquisite fabrics. Since 1988- we have set out to provide our sartorially inclined and discerning clientele with elegant masterpieces for gentlemen. Because of his The proprietor of this very craft house Md. Farooque has had more than four decades of experience in this domain. He started his journey from Dhanbad. Then thirst for perfection leads him to Mumbai and Dubai. But finally he settled down to Patna and became very successful because of his craftsmanship. Well-trained assistants and his seamless craftsmanship form the central pillar of the business. His artistry is acclaimed by various affluents.
